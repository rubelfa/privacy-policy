We may collect non-personal information, including but not limited to device type, operating system, usage data, and app performance data. This information helps us improve our Apps and optimize your experience.

Some of our Apps may collect location data to provide location-based services or for analytics purposes. You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device settings.

For apps with financial features (like budget management or transaction tracking), we may collect transaction data such as category, amount, and payment methods. This data is used only within the app and stored securely.

We use your information for the following purposes:

These third-party services may have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the app’s services or comply with legal obligations. Non-personal data may be retained for analytical purposes.

6. Your Choices

You have control over your personal information:

  • Access and Update: You can view and update your account information within the app settings.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt-out of receiving notifications or ads by adjusting your device or app settings.
  • Delete Account: You can request to delete your account and associated data by contacting us at the email provided below.
